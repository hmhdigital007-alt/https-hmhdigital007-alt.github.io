STRATEGIC FLOW

Design Process.

Step 01

Niche Research

Utilizing proprietary filters to inspect high-volume, low-competition passion search terms on Amazon, Etsy, and Redbubble. Checking forums and trending hashtags to locate target community pain points.

Step 02

Concept & Theme

Developing specific typography layouts, matching visual color boards to current trending palettes, and outlining illustrative themes tailored precisely to the researched community demographic.

Step 03

Raw Sketching

Hand-drawing ideas, constructing grid lines, and testing initial structural balance. Choosing font combinations and arranging graphic elements to maximize shelf-appeal on actual shirts.

Step 04

Digital Design

Assembling vectors in Adobe Illustrator or complex compositions in Photoshop. Ensuring flawless utilization of negative space, vector cleaning, and optimizing print boundaries.

Step 05

Contrast Review

In-house digital inspection testing the design transparency across 15+ popular garment colorways (Asphalt, Heather, Black, Navy, Slate, etc.) to guarantee high readability and premium print contrast.

Step 06

Final Artwork

Exporting industry-standard, print-ready high-resolution files (4500x5400 px, 300 DPI, sRGB PNG) with clean alpha channels, optimized for Direct-to-Garment (DTG) printing setups worldwide.
